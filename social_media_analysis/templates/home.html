Analytics by Network

Twitter

We gather the requested user's profile and latest tweets. We analyze the tweet's contents and tell you about topics usage in form of "tag clouds" so that you can easily understand which words were the most popular.

We also show you things not shown on public Twitter pages, such as join date, timezone and followers ratio. With Foller.me it's very easy and incredibly fast to make up your mind on whether to follow somebody or not!

Facebook

We analyze the given advertisement details, predict the number of clicks you might get for your advertisement. Also provide some tips for increasing the number of clicks by changing some parameters, such as; tergeted gender, weekday and the ad spends.
We analyze the comments of your post and show each of them as positive, negative or neutral. Comments are analysed based on the text part and also the imojies.

YouTube

We gather information about requested channels or videos. We analyze their content, details and statistics so you can easily compare them with yours or many others.
We also show you things not shown on public channel pages and video descriptions, such as joined date,category,status and view ratio. With SM analysis it's very easy and incredibly fast to decide what to do, how to do and when to do on social media!

Stack Overflow

Stack Overflow is a question and answer site for professional and enthusiast programmers.
This is an StackOverflow analytics application that gives you rich insights about any public StackOverflow profile. We generates your Stack Profile Questions details and give a full analyze about your Maturity of posting a question, then calculate overall maturity and Technology usage on Stack overflow you used to post.!
